NP Membership Benefits

As a non-profit organization, you gain access to our community with no membership fee. Enjoy our support through shared resources, community events, and networking opportunities. NP Members can also apply for specific grants and funding programs.

×

Bronze Membership Benefits

Bronze Members receive all NP Member benefits plus access to exclusive webinars, networking events, and priority registration for certain events. This membership level is perfect for smaller organizations seeking to expand their reach.

×

Silver Membership Benefits

Silver Members enjoy all the benefits of Bronze Membership, with additional perks including discounted rates for advertising, premium event access, and early access to workshops and training sessions. This membership is ideal for organizations looking for more visibility and growth opportunities.

×

Gold Membership Benefits

Gold Members receive top-tier benefits, including all Silver Membership advantages, plus featured advertising on our homepage, VIP access to exclusive events, one-on-one consultations with experts, and free registration for special events and training programs. This is the ultimate membership level for organizations looking to maximize their influence.
